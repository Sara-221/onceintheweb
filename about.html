About

Once in the Web is a small Web Design and Development agency with a focus on sustainable design. Founded by two sisters: Sara, experienced in web development and full front end design, and Paula, illustrator and art director.
The project borns from the honest desire of making beautiful, useful and impactful websites that do not damage the planet. In Once in the Web, we believe that creativity and conciousness can come together to create positive change and inspire a green future.

Our Principle

Web technology currently produces 3.8% of global carbon emissions. We all need to reduce the amount of energy we use per day, but Once in The Web also wants the world to know and understand your project.

Our small digital agency helps private individuals and small companies to create a quality presence online while limiting their carbon footprint.

For that, we conceive simple but unique eco-friendly websites.

If your wish is to build a sustainable website, or your project tackles climate change, let's work together on it.

An eco-friendly website is the one that optimizes its design and development processes in order to reduce global carbon emissions. It commits to greener web practices and usually adopt the Sustainable Web Manifesto.

In the web, sustainable development is an environment-first approach that respects the principles of The Sustainable Web Manifesto. This means that our webs are created chasing minimum environmental impact while using performance-based web and usability standards that prioritize business goals and users' needs.
