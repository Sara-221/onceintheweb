The Website Conception Process

1

Discovery

  • We discuss your goals and needs.
  • We get to know your audience.
  • We deliver user journeys, content hierarchy and sitemap.
2

Web Design

  • We make sure that our design conveys your message exactly in the way you want it.
  • We deliver wireframes, moodboards and visual UI design.
  • We prototype the solution.
  • We iterate until the design feels yours.
3

Development

  • We take care of technical planning.
  • We develop the website in the programming language that suits your application best.
  • We focus on reducing page weights through assets optimisation and efficient web practices to lower the website CO2 emission per visit.
  • We deliver a fully responsive, custom designed website.
4

Training

  • We train your team to modify and add new content.
  • We help you set up any other features you would like to learn about.
  • We test the website, to ensure optimal performance.
  • We propose on-going support (optional).
